About

Currently studying as a second-year Master student in the Physics faculty at Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, I have always had an interest in IT and web development. I have taken part in the IT Generation program to have a possibility to participate in a heads-on course which could open doors for my further development as a front-end developer.

check_circle My goals
My main goal is to learn basics and more advanced tecniques of working with CSS, Javascript and HTML.
check_circle My dreams
